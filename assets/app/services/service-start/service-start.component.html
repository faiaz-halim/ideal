Full Service

Having a full service on your vehicle, includes : topping up of the brake fluid and coolant, brake checks, inspection of the engine, wheel bearings and shock absorbers, replacement of pollen filters and inspection of the air conditioning system.

The cost of a full service usually includes everything apart from any parts specified as requiring replacement in your vehicle's servicing schedule, such as fuel filters, spark plugs etc. These can be added as chargeable extras.

Pink Slips

An eSafety (or pink slip) inspection report verifies that your vehicle is safe and ready for the road. Most light vehicles more than 5 years old will require an eSafety check before they can be registered in NSW. Your registration renewal papers will state whether or not you need an inspection.

We will send the result of the test directly to Roads and Maritime Services.

To know more about eSafety checks , visit our eSafety (Pink slip) check page.
